# Final publication results

The four subdirectories are the authoritative publication packages for Experiments 1–4. They contain final tables, figures, methodology notes, captions, and supporting machine-readable outputs.

Complete raw Experiment 3 timing and ColorWheel comparison data are stored under `experiments/experiment_3/raw_results/`.

The complete Experiment 2 masks are stored once under the repository-level `masks/` directory.

The Experiment 4 supporting archive is redacted: the six raw AFM case-study images were removed, while generated masks, JSON/CSV records, disagreement maps, route notes, and downstream outputs were retained.
