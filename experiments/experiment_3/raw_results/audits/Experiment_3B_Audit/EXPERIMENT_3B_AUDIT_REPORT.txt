The 49-image timing script completed and wrote all expected files.

Voronoi
-------
- Applicable images: 22
- Completed images: 22/22 (100%)
- Completed repetitions: 66/66 (100%)
- Corrected image-level mean: 13.629 s
- Corrected image-level median: 13.722 s

The original downstream_timing_summary.csv reports n=15 for Voronoi because it
discarded successful Voronoi measurements whenever a later ColorWheel stage
timed out. That is an aggregation error. The corrected Voronoi sample size is 22.

Original ColorWheel
-------------------
- Routed images: 29
- Images completing all three repetitions: 7/29 (24.1%)
- Images timing out in all three repetitions: 22/29 (75.9%)
- Successful repetitions: 21/87 (24.1%)
- Timeout repetitions: 66/87 (75.9%)
- All failures were 120-second TimeoutExpired events.

Among only the seven cases that completed:
- Mean: 58.816 s
- Median: 36.919 s
- Range: 22.603–100.545 s

These completed-case ColorWheel times are censored and biased toward faster
images. They must not be presented as representative of all ColorWheel-routed
images.

Publication interpretation
--------------------------
Voronoi timing is valid and publication-usable. The original ColorWheel does
not have a valid general runtime estimate under this protocol because 75.9% of
routed images failed to complete within 120 seconds. The completion rate and
timeout rate are valid robustness/scalability results.

The overall downstream totals in downstream_timing_summary.csv are not valid
as a complete 49-image end-to-end average because they exclude 22 timed-out
images.
