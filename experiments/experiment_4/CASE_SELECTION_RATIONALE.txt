EXPERIMENT 4 CASE-SELECTION RATIONALE
=====================================

Representative successes
------------------------
The dots, lines, mixed, and irregular examples are the images nearest the
median Dice score within each true morphology class in Experiment 2. They were
chosen before running Experiment 4 and were not selected for visually favorable
downstream outputs.

Retained failures
-----------------
Mixed_01 is retained because it had the lowest Dice score in the complete
segmentation validation set. It tests whether a poor predicted mask produces
technically complete but potentially misleading downstream results.

lines_01 is retained because it was a true line morphology classified as
irregular with confidence greater than 0.9999. Under the final routing rules,
that classification suppresses ColorWheel execution. This case demonstrates
that end-to-end system behavior depends on upstream classification accuracy.

No manual correction
--------------------
Experiment 4 does not introduce a corrected mask. This avoids mixing a human
intervention study into the qualitative software demonstration.
